About us

Paltan Games is a team of passionate, creative people who take great pleasure in coming up with new and unique ideas and have the determination to keep working until the idea has been implemented.

It consists of people who are passionate not just about creating games, but also about playing games, which gives them a wider thinking field. Here, we believe in small teams where everyone has a great contribution in bringing a game to life.

At Paltan Games, we appreciate great ideas, even if it fails, in terms of implementation or in terms of gaining popularity of the gamers. We believe in creating addicting, simple games which one can play whenever he wants.
